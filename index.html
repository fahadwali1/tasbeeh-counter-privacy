Privacy Policy for Tasbeeh Counter

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Tasbeeh Counter ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection and Storage

Local Data Storage Only

Tasbeeh Counter operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

What Data is Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Tasbeeh counts: Daily counts for each tasbeeh (SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar, Astaghfirullah)
  • Historical data: Daily counts stored for up to 365 days
  • Statistics: Lifetime totals, streaks, and progress data
  • Settings: Haptic feedback preferences and daily goals

All data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted outside your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since all data remains on your device:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is used for advertising
  • No data is sold to anyone

Third-Party Services

Tasbeeh Counter does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The app operates completely independently without any external data collection services.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Vibrate permission (Android): Used only for haptic feedback when you tap the counter. This permission is optional and can be disabled in settings.

No other permissions are required or requested by the app.

Children's Privacy

Tasbeeh Counter is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app complies with all children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security
  • Data is only accessible through the app on your device
  • If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted
  • We recommend backing up your device regularly if you want to preserve your data

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  1. Uninstalling the app from your device
  2. Clearing app data through your device's settings

Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered as it was never transmitted to any external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, you have complete control over:

  • Your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Whether to use the app
  • Whether to delete the app and all its data

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): No personal data is collected or processed
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): No personal information is sold or shared
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act): Safe for children, no data collection

Summary

In simple terms:

  • All your data stays on your device
  • We don't collect anything
  • We don't track you
  • We don't share anything
  • We don't use ads or analytics
  • Your privacy is completely protected

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all versions of the Tasbeeh Counter app.
