Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my counters?

To reset a counter, go to the counter screen and tap the reset button (usually located at the bottom or in the settings). This will reset the current day's count for that specific tasbeeh.

How do I view my statistics?

Tap on the "Stats" tab in the app to view your daily, weekly, monthly, and all-time statistics. You can see charts and graphs showing your progress over time.

Can I set daily goals?

Yes! You can set daily goals for each tasbeeh. Go to Settings to customize your daily goals for each counter.

Is my data stored online?

No, all your data is stored locally on your device. The app works completely offline and doesn't require an internet connection. Your privacy is protected.

How do I backup my data?

Since all data is stored locally, you can backup your data by backing up your device using iCloud (iOS) or Google Backup (Android). If you delete the app, your data will be lost unless you have a device backup.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Currently, the app stores data locally on each device. Data is not synced between devices. Each device will have its own separate counters and statistics.

How do I change the haptic feedback settings?

Go to Settings in the app and toggle the haptic feedback option on or off according to your preference.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any bugs or issues with the app, please contact us at contact@sussexendocrineclinic.co.uk with the following information:

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Your device model and operating system version
  • App version (found in Settings)
  • Screenshots if applicable
